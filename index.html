Tips for a strong report

📱

Phone number is most important. Even if you don't have the account number, a phone number is enough to flag someone.

🔢

Account name matters. Fraudsters reuse names across accounts — it helps us link related reports.

📝

Be specific in your description. The AI uses your words to explain the tactic to others — the more detail, the better the warning.

🔒

Your identity is never shown. Reports are posted anonymously. We only store what you type here.
